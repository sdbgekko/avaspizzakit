What’s in the box

Everything to make one pizza the TJ’s way

Our San Marzano Sauce

TJ’s scratch-made red sauce — sweet, slow-simmered tomatoes and herbs — in a ready-to-spread cup. Spooning and swirling is the kids’ favorite part.

Our story

37 years on the beach — now in your kitchen

Since 1989, TJ’s Italian Café has been one block from the sand in Indian Rocks Beach, feeding generations of families the same scratch-made recipes. Kids who once stood on a step-stool to watch the dough get tossed now bring their own kids in for pizza night.

We named the kit after Ava — the youngest of our family — because the best part of this place was always the little hands helping in the kitchen.

Ava’s Pizza Kit packs that tradition into a box and sends it to your table. Same dough, same sauce, same cheese — made by your family, in your kitchen, with flour on every cheek. Because some things are worth passing down, one pizza night at a time.
